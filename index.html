Cutting board

Sharp knife (adult needed)

Large plate Measuring cups Blender (adult needed) 2 glasses

INSTRUCTIONS

Put the watermelon cubes in a single layer on the plate and freeze until solid, about 2 hours. Put the frozen watermelon in the blender, then add the strawberries, yogurt, and cold water. Put the top on tightly. Turn on the blender to medium speed and blend until the mixture is smooth, 30 to 60 seconds. Pour the smoothie into the glasses and serve right away, or cover and refrigerate up to 4 hours.
